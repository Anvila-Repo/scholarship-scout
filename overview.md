# Overview

## Mission
To make financial aid opportunities discoverable and engaging. Scholarship Scout serves as a high-velocity content engine that builds trust and audience growth on social channels by consistently delivering high-value, verified, actionable opportunities directly to followers.

## Core Capabilities
- Daily automated web-research and scraping of new scholarship listings.
- Link health checks and validation patterns.
- Platform-specific social media copywriting.
- Scheduled content queuing and draft management.
